2. Description

The Toyota Supra is a legendary high-performance sports car combining sleek design, precision handling, and raw power. Famous for its modding potential and pop culture legacy, especially the Mk4 (2JZ), it’s a favorite among gearheads and collectors alike.

3. Origins

First launched in 1978 as a Celica variant, the Supra quickly evolved into its own iconic line. The 1993–2002 Mk4 earned a legendary status in tuning and racing communities. The 2019 rebirth (A90) blended modern design with German tech, reviving the legend.

5. Maintenance & Reliability

Older Supras (Mk3/Mk4) are robust but may require more maintenance with age. The A90 is modern and reliable, though its BMW-based internals may lead to higher servicing costs. Routine care ensures long-term performance.

6. Global Popularity

The Supra has a massive following across the U.S., Japan, and Europe. From racing simulators to car shows and drag strips, it's a cult classic that continues to inspire generations of car lovers.

7. Fun Facts

  • The Mk4’s 2JZ-GTE engine can handle over 1000 hp with stock internals.
  • The Supra from Fast & Furious made it a pop culture icon.
  • The new Supra (A90) was co-developed with BMW (shares platform with the Z4).
